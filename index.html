About the Studio

A one-person studio with five furry co-founders

Five Cats Studios is an independent game development studio founded in 2026 by Nathan Barkley in Grundy County, Iowa. We build cozy, systems-rich games that give players room to breathe, discover, and settle in for the long haul.

Our flagship title, Smoke & Honey, is a beekeeping farm simulation built in Godot 4 with handcrafted pixel art. It blends realistic apiary management with warm NPC relationships, seasonal rhythms, and a peaceful Iowa small-town setting.

We believe games can be both deep and gentle. Our work is rooted in Iowa's agricultural heritage, a love of living systems, and the conviction that the best games teach through wonder, not punishment.

Smoke & Honey

A Cozy Beekeeping Farm Sim

Your uncle keeps bees on the edge of Cedar Bend, Iowa — population 1,847, one stoplight, zero strangers — and he needs your help. Manage Langstroth and Warre hives, breed queens, craft honey and mead, and become part of a small town where everyone has a history and help arrives before the need is stated. Twelve characters. Five years. No fail states. Only time, patience, and the slow hum of a healthy hive.

Latest Updates

April 3, 2026 LATEST
Feature
Quest engine launched with Year 1 chain: Five quests auto-chain from First Light through Battening Down, with month-gating, event hooks from inspections and harvests, and a compound winter-ready check requiring 60+ lbs of honey plus a completed mite wash.
Feature
Knowledge Journal and custom hive naming: New two-tab journal system with 16 unlockable notebook entries and per-hive inspection records. Hives can now be given custom names. A Keen Observer bonus tracks how often the player takes notes.
Feature
Alcohol wash mite monitoring mini-game: Five-step interactive process from frame selection through mite counting, with a scripted first-wash tutorial featuring Ellen's queen warning. Color-coded threshold display reports results to the quest system.
Feature
AFB two-state disease model: American Foulbrood now progresses from sub-clinical spores to visible clinical infection. Strong colonies (15k+ adults) can clear early infections at roughly 15% per week. Includes rope test diagnosis and hive destruction mechanics.
Feature
Winter hive observation system: During Deepcold and Kindlemonth, inspections now route to a passive observation panel showing weight, sound, and entrance activity with color-coded thresholds and contextual advice. Results auto-log to the Knowledge Journal.
