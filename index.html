About the Studio

A one-person studio with five furry co-founders

Five Cats Studios is an independent game development studio founded in 2026 by Nathan Barkley in Grundy County, Iowa. We build cozy, systems-rich games that give players room to breathe, discover, and settle in for the long haul.

Our flagship title, Smoke & Honey, is a beekeeping farm simulation built in Godot 4 with handcrafted pixel art. It blends realistic apiary management with warm NPC relationships, seasonal rhythms, and a peaceful Iowa small-town setting.

We believe games can be both deep and gentle. Our work is rooted in Iowa's agricultural heritage, a love of living systems, and the conviction that the best games teach through wonder, not punishment.

The studio runs lean, operates independently, and answers to no one but the five cats who sleep on the desk.

Smoke & Honey

A Cozy Beekeeping Farm Sim

Inherit a struggling apiary and rebuild it into a thriving bee farm. Manage Langstroth and Warre hives, breed queens, track seasonal forage, craft honey and mead, and forge lasting relationships with the residents of Cedar Bend, Iowa. There are no fail states. Only time, patience, and the slow hum of a healthy hive.

Development Log

What We Built Today

A live look at active development on Smoke & Honey. Updated as new features, fixes, and systems land in the codebase.

March 31, 2026 Today
Critical Fix
Persistent inventory system — Fixed a critical bug where player inventory was lost on every scene change. Inventory now mirrors to a GameData autoload so items survive entering buildings and interiors.
Redesign
All NPC shop & menu UIs rebuilt — Feed & Supply, Diner, Sell Screen, and Shop Screen completely rewritten with clickable buttons, mouse support, and proper viewport layouts. Fixed items rendering off-screen.
Feature
Quest system, beeswax tracking & winter warnings — Quest manager with daily tasks and XP rewards. Beeswax now tracked during scraping. Fall harvest warns if colony honey stores fall below 60 lbs.
Feature
Main menu, pause menu & character creator overhaul — Simplified start flow, new "Save & Exit" confirmation dialog on pause, and streamlined character creator down to three focus sections.
Feature
Hierarchical ESC key — ESC now backs out layer by layer: close UI overlay first, then exit building, then open pause menu. No more conflicting key handlers.
