Why Art Direction

Smoke & Honey's core systems (hive management, seasonal cycles, queen breeding, NPC relationships) are built and functional. The game plays. What it needs now is a cohesive visual identity that matches the depth of its mechanics.

This funding goes entirely toward art production because that is the single biggest gap between a working prototype and a game people want to play. The engine, design, and writing are handled by the developer. Art is the bottleneck.

Every dollar of this ask goes directly into the screen. No overhead, no office rent, no salaries. Just pixels.

Return Structure

Per Indie Fund's standard terms, Five Cats Studios will repay the investment from revenue generated by Smoke & Honey upon release. We are comfortable with a revenue-share arrangement and are open to discussing specific terms that work for both sides.
