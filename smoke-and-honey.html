What Is Smoke & Honey?

An apiary to inherit. A town to discover. A life to build.

You've inherited your uncle's struggling apiary on the edge of Cedar Bend, Iowa. The hives are half-empty, the equipment is worn, and the neighbors are curious. What you do with all of it is entirely up to you.

Smoke & Honey is a top-down pixel-art farming simulation where realistic beekeeping meets cozy farm life. Manage Langstroth and Warre hives, breed queens, track seasonal forage maps, and craft honey, beeswax candles, and mead. Build relationships with a cast of fully-realized Iowa small-town characters. Take your time. There is no timer. There is no losing.

Core Pillars

What makes it special

Four interlocking systems built around patience, discovery, and the slow rhythm of a beekeeping life.

🐝
Realistic Beekeeping

Manage Langstroth and Warre hives with genuine apiculture mechanics — queen genetics, brood cycles, seasonal forage maps, disease and pest management. The bees behave like bees.

🌻
Cozy Farm Life

Seasonal cycles, dynamic weather, and a full farm-building loop. Harvest honey, press beeswax candles, brew mead, and tend a rural homestead that grows at your pace.

💬
Rich NPC Relationships

Eight fully-voiced characters with deep quest arcs — 60 quest chains, 8,200+ XP, and stories woven through the community of Cedar Bend. Relationships built over seasons, not minutes.

🕊️
Peaceful Progression

No fail states. No timer. No punishment for stepping away. Smoke & Honey teaches through discovery and rewards curiosity, patience, and care over efficiency and optimization.

In the Hives

A living apiary system

Queen Breeding Selectively breed queens for traits like honey production, gentleness, and disease resistance. Genetics matter — and they carry forward through generations.
Seasonal Forage Maps Bees forage differently across spring, summer, and fall. Know your landscape, plant the right flowers, and watch productivity shift with the seasons.
Health & Pest Management Monitor for Varroa mites, nosema, and hive beetles. Intervene with treatments, or let nature run its course. Balance is everything.
Hive Expansion Grow from a single Langstroth to a full apiary of Langstroth and Warre hives. Each hive type has its own management style and character.
Honey & Wax Crafting Extract raw honey, filter it into grades, pour beeswax candles, and brew wildflower mead. Every product has a market and a story.
Farm Building Design and construct your homestead — honey house, potting shed, wildflower meadow, market stall. Build at your own pace, your own way.
Iowa Seasons & Weather Real seasonal rhythms with dynamic weather — spring rains, summer heat, autumn color, winter stillness. The land breathes around you.
Educational Depth Every mechanic is grounded in real apiculture. Smoke & Honey teaches beekeeping through play, not textbooks.
