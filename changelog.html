FIX SIMULATION FIX: Honey supers never filling -- 4 bottlenecks found and fixed. (1) LBS_PER_NU was 0.0005 (10x too low). At peak summer NU ~800/day, colony produced only 0.15 lbs/day gross -- less than the 0.5 lbs/day consumption. Net production was NEGATIVE, making it physically impossible to fill supers. Fixed: LBS_PER_NU = 0.005. Now at peak: 400 NU -> 2.0 lbs/day gross, ~1.5 net after consumption. Over 80 productive days = ~120 lbs gross, ~80 lbs harvestable (enough for 2 supers). (2) Super comb drawing rate was 0.30 per house bee with 3000 cell/day cap -- far too slow. A 2450-cell super frame side would take days to draw even at max rate. Real bees draw a full super in 1-2 weeks during peak flow. Fixed: base rate 0.80 per house bee, cap raised to 8000 cells/day. (3) Super comb drawing required honey_stores >= 2.0 lbs and brood box >= 80% drawn. Both thresholds were too conservative -- lowered to 1.0 lbs stores and 60% brood drawn. store_mult scaling improved: starts at 0.50 at 1 lb (was 0.30 at 2 lbs). (4) Season rankings forced to S-rank as dev default for tuning. Random roll commented out with TODO to restore once production is balanced.
